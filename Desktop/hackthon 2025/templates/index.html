Tools for Visually Impaired & Cognitive Learners

Welcome to VisualCogn. A website for accessible learning and document processing.

Keyboard Shortcuts

File Operations

  • U – Upload file
  • E – Extract text

Processing

  • S – Summarize text
  • R – Read aloud
  • D – Download MP3

Settings

  • + – Increase font size
  • - – Decrease font size
  • D – Toggle dark mode
  • Q – Toggle quiet mode
  • H – Show this help

Step 1: Upload Your File

Upload a PDF, image, or text file. We'll extract the text for you.

Supported: PDF, images (PNG, JPG), TXT files. Max 50MB.

Step 2: Extract Text

Get text from your uploaded file.

Step 3: Process Your Text

For Easier Understanding (Summary)

A shorter version of your text for easier reading.

Listen to Your Text (Text to Speech)

Hear your text read aloud. You can adjust the reading speed.

1.0x

Download as Audio File

Save your text as an MP3 audio file to listen later.

You can play the MP3 file on any device.

Step 4: Speech to Braille (Optional)

Record your voice and convert it to Braille format.

Supported: MP3, WAV, and other common audio formats.
